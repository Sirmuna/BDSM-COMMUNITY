Welcome to BDSM COMMUNITY

Consent Meets Connection.

The BDSM Community is a highly respected and authentic platform created to foster safe, consensual, and meaningful connections between verified Dominatrix and genuine submissives. Since its official launch in 2020, the community has grown into a trusted global network that champions the values of trust, honesty, love, sincerity, and mutual respect. It was founded by the National BDSM Community Board with the vision of creating a safe space where individuals interested in the BDSM lifestyle can explore their desires without fear, judgment, or exploitation.

The BDSM lifestyle is deeply rooted in consensual power exchange, emotional and physical trust, and the freedom to express one’s kinks and fetishes in a secure environment. However, as the popularity of BDSM grew, so did the number of individuals misusing the term and space for malicious purposes. Reports of fraud, blackmail, harassment, and sexual assault began to surface, threatening not only individual safety but also the reputation and core values of the community.

Recognizing the urgent need to protect and preserve the integrity of BDSM, a collective of Dominatrix and submissives came together in 2019 to form a strong, united front. That initiative laid the foundation for what is now known as the BDSM Community. This organization was created to provide structure, accountability, and protection for members of the lifestyle, ensuring that every interaction is guided by transparency, respect, and ethical standards.
